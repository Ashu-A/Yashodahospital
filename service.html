Services

Excellent Medical Services

Emergency Care

We provide 24/7 emergency services with a dedicated team of doctors and medical staff ready to handle critical conditions. Our well-equipped emergency department ensures rapid diagnosis and immediate treatment for patients in need of urgent care.

Operation & Surgery

Our hospital offers advanced surgical care, including general and specialized procedures performed by experienced surgeons. With modern operation theatres and cutting-edge technology, we ensure safe and successful surgeries for our patients.

Outdoor Checkup

We offer comprehensive outpatient services, allowing patients to consult with expert physicians and specialists for routine checkups, follow-ups, and preventive healthcare. Our doctors provide personalized care to diagnose and treat various medical conditions.

Ambulance Service

Our ambulance service is available 24/7, ensuring fast and safe transportation for patients in critical condition. We are committed to providing quick response times and expert pre-hospital care to save lives.

Gynecology

We provide expert care for women's health, including prenatal care, reproductive health, and treatment for gynecological conditions, ensuring comprehensive and compassionate medical support.

Blood Testing

We provide a wide range of diagnostic blood tests, including routine screenings and specialized tests, ensuring accurate and timely results to aid in diagnosis and treatment planning.
